Surviving from Land Slides - Guidelines

Before the Land Slides:

  • Be aware of the signs of an impending landslide, such as ground cracks and tilting trees..
  • Identify the areas prone to landslides in your vicinity and avoid building or living in those areas.
  • Ensure proper drainage and slope stabilization measures on your property.
  • Stay informed about weather conditions and heed evacuation orders or warnings.

During the Land Slides:

  • If indoors, move to a safe location on the ground floor away from windows and doors.
  • If outdoors, move to higher ground and avoid steep slopes and areas near rivers or streams.
  • Do not attempt to cross or drive through areas that are prone to landslides.
  • Stay tuned to local news or emergency alerts for updates and instructions.

After the Land Slides:

  • Stay away from the affected area until it has been deemed safe by authorities.
  • Check for any damage to your property and report it to the relevant authorities or insurance company.
  • Avoid entering damaged buildings or structures as they may be unstable.
  • Be cautious of potential secondary hazards such as flooding or gas leaks.
